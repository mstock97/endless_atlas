Japan · Kantō Region

Where ancient ritual meets neon overload.

Tokyo doesn't ease you in. It swallows you whole — 14 million people, 23 wards, and a rail network so precise they apologize when a train arrives 30 seconds late.

Spend a morning at Tsukiji's outer market before the city wakes. Get lost in Shimokitazawa's record shops and jazz bars. Watch the Shibuya crossing from above at midnight and understand, finally, why people call this place electric.
