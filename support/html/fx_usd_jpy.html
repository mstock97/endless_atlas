[Click to Edit] Japan is an island country in East Asia, located in the northwest Pacific Ocean. It is renowned for its harmonious blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge technology. From the neon-lit streets of Tokyo to the serene temples of Kyoto and the majestic peaks of Mount Fuji, the archipelago offers a unique tapestry of culture, nature, and history.
